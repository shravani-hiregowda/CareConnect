About CareConnect

A post-discharge patient care platform. We recognized the challenges healthcare providers face in monitoring patient recovery after hospital discharge and the anxiety patients experience during this critical period.

Our platform brings together cutting-edge technology with healthcare expertise to create a seamless experience for both nurses and patients. We're committed to improving patient outcomes through better communication, monitoring, and timely interventions.
